Bio

I received BS in 2011, MS in 2013 and PhD in 2019 at School of Electrical Engineering form KAIST, South Korea. The title of my dissertation (composed of four chapters, all published) was: Deep Learning Based Visual Recognition Robust Against Background Clusters, written under the supervision of Prof. In So Kweon. During the PhD course, I co-founded Lunit, a Seoul-based medical AI startup, with my lifelong friends in 2013. As the Chief of Research at Lunit, I am devoted to developing advanced medical AI for radiology and pathology. During my internship experience at Adobe Research in the US, I worked on large-scale video representation learning. My research interests include visual recognition problems with deep learning approaches.
